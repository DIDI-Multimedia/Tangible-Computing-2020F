Week 1 Update

An artist that I can relate to is Jaden Christopher Syre Smith

Jaden‘s music has always uplifted me and inspired me to create. Jaden released an album ‘SYRE’ and made a story based on his album The album is loosely based around the life narrative of a boy as he comes to age and figures out his own legacy The message behind his album was to leave a mark on people’s thinking of him and to think his the person that is always giving back to something or some one . Jaden and I are around the same age and want to deliver the same message I find it reliving to have to share same ambitions . Throughout time I would realize how strongly affected I am by this as I tend to make song lyrics as daily quotes to keep me moving . I base my paintings off of music I listen to I enjoy every minute of it and I’m looking forward for my next painting.
