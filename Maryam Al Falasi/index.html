MuW201 Tangible Computing

Maryam Al Falasi

This website was created by Maryam Al Falasi,
a second year Multimedia and Strategic Design
Management student at the Dubai Institute of
Design and Innovation (DIDI), for the MuW201
(Multimedia Workshop) Tangible Computing course.
Throughout this website will be a thorough
documentation of all of the works and projects
that have been created and completed throughout
the 2020 fall Semester. In this website I will
also be discussing various topics that exist in
the digital and physical art world and show all
the works that I have created throughout my time
in this course, I will mostly fixate on digital
media.
